Welcome to Smart Terrarium!

We're a team of tech enthusiasts passionate about reptiles. Our mission is to provide reptile owners with an intelligent terrarium management solution.

Smart Terrarium utilizes the ESP32 microcontroller to revolutionize reptile habitats. With our system, you can effortlessly monitor and control crucial factors like temperature, humidity, and lighting.

Our user-friendly interface offers real-time data visualization through charts and graphs. Stay informed about temperature trends and other metrics to ensure your reptiles thrive.
